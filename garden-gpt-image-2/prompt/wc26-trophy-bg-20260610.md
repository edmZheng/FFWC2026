# WC26 奖杯纪念徽记重绘（黑底欢迎页半透明背景用）

## 推荐用法

把原图（E:\Downloads\fifa_logo.png）上传给 ChatGPT / GPT-Image 作为参考图，配合下面 prompt 做"风格重绘"，比纯文字生成更还原构图。

## Prompt（直接复制）

Reinterpret the attached emblem as an original artistic homage — do NOT copy it exactly. Composition: a tall World Cup style golden trophy (abstract sculpted figures holding a globe) standing in front of two huge stacked rounded numerals "2" above "6", creating a vertical monogram.

Rendering style: luxurious dark monochrome relief. The numerals are rendered in very dark charcoal gray (#1A1A1A) with subtle glossy edge highlights, barely separating from a pure black #000000 background. The trophy is rendered in muted antique gold with soft metallic sheen, low saturation, dim museum lighting — elegant, not flashy.

No text anywhere: no "FIFA", no "World Cup" lettering, no TM mark, no watermark. Clean silhouette, centered, generous black margin around. Designed to be used as a faint watermark on a black app screen.

Background: pure solid black #000000.

Output: 1024x1536 (portrait), PNG.
